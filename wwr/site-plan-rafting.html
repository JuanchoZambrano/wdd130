Overview

Purpose

Here, the people who love extreme sports will find a company which take care of their clients and at the same time, offers them the opportunity to practice or live what rafting is about. This page was made for helping them to find a really good plan that suits their needs online, also as a company, the website will help us to improve our scope and people will be able to find us quickly.

Audience

Who are the target customers? People who love to feel adrenaline and are looking for a Rafting plan for their holidays and weekends. The target customers are people from twelve years old until fifty years old. If they don't have enough money, as a company we will try to adjust the plan to their budget. How will my audience usually access my site? The website is designed to show all our information in every smart device with access to websites. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? They will find everything related to the different plans we offer, like how much do they are, the things they can do, a short description and review of rafting rules, the payment methods and all sites we will use for practice this sport.
